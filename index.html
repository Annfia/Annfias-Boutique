Twitch Toolkit

TwitchToolkit is a mod written by Twitch.tv/hodlhodl that integrates storytelling decisions into chat votes, awards viewers coins for watching, and those coins can be spent on items/events in game.

  • !bal - check balance and karma rate
  • !buy- Syntax: !buy beer 6 - purchase an item or event
  • !price [quantity]- Syntax: Price Check
  • !whatiskarma - explains what the karma system is
  • !buylist - gives users a link to the public purchase list
  • !instructions - gives users info on using the mod
  • !modinfo - gives users info about the mod
  • !installedmods - Full list of installed active mods in game.
  • !modsettings - gives user info on how many coins are being earned and global settings
  • !colonists - Colonists Count
  • !wealth - Colony Wealth
  • !giftcoins - Syntax: !giftcoins @username 100 - give another viewer your coins
  • !mypawnstory - view information about your pawns backstory
  • !mypawnskills - view information about your pawns skills
  • !mypawngear - Shows an overview of your pawn's gear.
  • !mypawnbody - Shows an overview of your pawn's body.
  • !mypawnkills - Viewer kill count
  • !levelskill [skill] [coins] - Lets you wager coins to level up a skill of your choosing.
  • !passionshuffle [skill] - Shuffles your passions. If a skill is specified, you'll be guaranteed a minor passion in it.
  • !surgery [item] [quantity/bodypart] - Purchases the part specified and queues it for surgery on your pawn.
  • !trait [trait] - Replace with desired trait to add. Up to 4 traits total.
  • !removetrait [trait] - Replace with desired trait to remove. Up to 4 traits total.
  • !replacetrait [old] [new] - Replaces a trait with a different trait.
  • !passion [skill] - Upgrades a skill's passion... sometimes.
  • !lookup [item/category] [query] - Shows the first few results for the query in the given category. Valid categories are: 'pawns', 'traits', 'items', 'events', 'diseases', 'animals', and 'skills'."
