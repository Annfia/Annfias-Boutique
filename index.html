TwitchToolkit

TwitchToolkit is a mod written by Twitch.tv/hodlhodl that integrates storytelling decisions into chat votes, awards viewers coins for watching, and those coins can be spent on items/events in game.

  • !bal - Shows your current balance, karma, and optionally, the amount of coins earned per cycle.
  • !purchaselist
  • !giftcoins - Gives a viewer some of your coins.
  • !joinqueue
  • !buy
  • !levelskill - Lets you wager coins to level up a skill of your choosing.
  • !modsettings
  • !allcommands
  • !installedmods - Shows you the complete list of installed mods.
  • !setfavoritecolor - Sets the user's pawn's favorite color to the color specified. The color can be a name or a hexcode.
  • !setfavouritecolour - Sets the user's pawn's favourite colour to the colour specified. The colour can be a name or a hex code.
  • !reviveall
  • !reviveme
  • !healme
  • !healall
  • !passionshuffle [skill] - Shuffles your passions. If a skill is specified, you'll be guaranteed a minor passion in it.
  • !surgery - Purchases the part specified and queues it for surgery on your pawn.
  • !trait
  • !removetrait
  • !reviveanypawn
  • !healanypawn
  • !replacetrait - Replaces a trait with a different trait.
  • !backpack [quantity] - It's like buy item, but it gets added to your inventory if possible.
  • !removepassion - Downgrades a skill's passion... sometimes.
  • !passion - Upgrades a skill's passion... sometimes.
  • !fullheal
  • !rescueme
  • !mypawnkills
  • !mypawnstats [stat1] [stat2] - Shows your current stats within a given category.
  • !mypawnhealth [capacity] - Shows an overview of your pawn's health. If a capacity is specified, it'll show what's affecting it.
  • !mypawnneeds
  • !mypawngear - Shows an overview of your pawn's gear.
  • !mypawnbody
  • !mypawnstory
  • !mypawnskills
  • !fixmypawn
  • !leave - Makes your pawn leave the colony.
  • !mypawnrelations [viewer] - Shows your pawn's relationships to other viewers.
  • !factions
  • !research [project/item] - Shows the current research progress. If a project is specified, it'll show the progress of that instead.
  • !lookup [query] - Shows the first few results for the query in the given category. Valid categories are: 'pawns', 'traits', 'items', 'events', 'diseases', 'animals', and 'skills'.
  • !price [query] - Similar to the lookup command, except it'll show the price for the first valid match.
  • !insult [target] - Makes your pawn insult a random viewer. If a viewer is specified, you'll insult them instead.
  • !wealth
  • !colonists
  • !rwdata [weaponname] - Provides some information about supported things. Supported categories are: 'weapons'
  • !imstuck
  • !joinraid
